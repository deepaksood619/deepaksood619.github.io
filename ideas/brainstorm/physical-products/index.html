Physical Products & Hardware Startup Ideas

Marketplace & Platforms​

Consolidated Gig Marketplace​

Single platform for all delivery (customers and delivery agents - gig workers):

  • Zomato + Swiggy - Food delivery
  • DTDC + Amazon + ecommerce - Parcel delivery
  • Uber + Ola - Transportation
  • Dunzo + Porter - Adhoc delivery

Features: Dynamic pricing and price surge (like Uber/Ola)

Personalized Gym Trainers with Equipment at Home​

  • The only reason people go out is gyms
  • Or create good experiences at home:
    • Book candle-light dinner experience at home
    • Good chef cooks great food
    • Butlers come with all equipment
    • Restaurant on wheels

Why people don't want to go out:

  • Don't like seeing poor people
  • Don't like seeing dirty city
  • Depression/anxiety

Electronics / Electrical​

Consumer Electronics​

  • Socket without switch - Smart power management
  • Smart appliance for Indian cooking (like DelishUp)
    • Smart connected appliance
    • Pre-sets for cooking tasks
    • Integrated weighing scale
  • Live TV on phones without internet - Broadcast receiver
  • Smartphone with built-in earbuds (like Nokia 5710 but smartphone not feature phone)
  • Rechargeable appliances
    • Rechargeable LEDs, fans, LED tube lights
  • Wall/paint that doesn't need nails - Stick photos, clocks without drilling
    • Same concept for lights and wiring

IoT Devices​

IoT Water Tank Monitoring System​

  • Fully autonomous
  • Creates its own energy
  • Ball-shaped, floats inside tank
  • Measures water level using accelerometer
  • Sends data via WiFi to cloud
  • Cloud analytics with user dashboard
  • Must be cheap

RFID Smart Meters​

  • Users scan and send meter reading to server
  • Removes need for manual meter reading personnel
  • Pay units via app
  • Tamper-proof, locked from inside
  • ML detects anomalies for on-site verification

IoT Switch​

  • Smart switches for home automation

Automotive Electronics​

  • Side mirror safety - Sees behind before opening door
  • Fog cameras in cars - Night vision/LiDAR/radar to see through fog

Hardware & Engineering​

Mechanical​

Vertical Parking System for Cars + SUVs​

  • Max height for 15 cars
  • Each slot SUV-height compatible
  • Height checked at entrance
  • Time-based or weight-based pricing
  • Can be placed at metro stations
  • 5 structures = 75 cars in ground space of 5 cars
  • Steel truss structure for easy addition/removal
  • Modular construction

Energy​

  • Small nuclear power plants for cities
    • No transmission losses
    • No large-scale investment
    • No large-scale nuclear disaster
    • Smaller systems more stable

Other Mechanical​

  • Reusable flex - Reprinted digitally, no need to remove/reinstall
  • Carbon fiber products - Cars, train bogies, freight wagons, containers, homes
    • 5x stronger than steel, ultra-lightweight
  • Cool-weather clothing - Clothing with micro-electronics for cooling
    • Fans and cooling liquid to transfer heat off body

Civil Engineering & Infrastructure​

Construction​

  • Low-cost prefab and movable PGs (Paying Guest accommodations)
  • Better and Cheap Pervious Concrete
    • Porous concrete allows water/air passage
    • Preserves trees, reduces stormwater runoff
    • Challenge: Less tensile/flexural strength
    • Clogging issues to solve

Traffic Management​

Connected traffic lights with intelligent routing:

  • Computer vision traffic management
  • Single pole with high mast 4-side cameras
  • Traffic lights on same pole
  • VAC (Vehicle Actuated Control) - Adjusts timing based on vehicle presence
  • ATC (Adaptive Traffic Control)
  • AI streamlines flow using aerial photographs

References:

  • What does ATC or VAC mean on traffic signals?
  • Time-based maps instead of space

  • Scheduling capability
  • Group multiple appliances
  • Cost saving: No switch boards, only sockets

Mobile as Laptop​
