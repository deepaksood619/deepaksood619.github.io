Miscellaneous SaaS & Product Ideas

Core SaaS Principles​

Create best software companies with Indian labor - Since labor is cheap in India (like Zoho)

Don't pay for bits, pay for atoms - Software is free/open source, pay for physical infrastructure and usage

Problem with Current SaaS Pricing​

  • Per-user based (inefficient)
  • Should be usage-based instead
    • Pay for 1 GB storage + 1 CPU + 2 GB RAM exactly
    • Plus some revenue margin

Developer tools - pure software play

Niche & Micro-SaaS​

Weekend Jobs Platform​

  • List jobs only for weekends
  • Full hiring platform specifically for weekend work

Project Management​

  • Project management is broken - Need AI project manager
  • Features: reports, deadlines, utilization reports
  • Finding overwork and underwork
  • Most efficient utilization of resources

Government & Civic​

Government Projects Tracker​

  • Single place where all government projects announced are listed
  • Track: due dates, on-track status
  • Features: Search, visual timelines, updates, state bifurcation, tags
  • Follow Twitter handles and news to update project status
  • Reference: https://www.newprojectstracker.com/
  • Also: Government corruption tracker

Lie Detector​

  • Whatever politicians say, make it searchable
  • Transcribe and add summary with full transcript
  • Highlight contradictory statements

Productivity & Tools​

Multimodal Transportation Cost Explorer​

  • Compare costs across different transport modes

Redis for Notification​

  • Notification system infrastructure

Thoughts & Observations​

  • Why doesn't Google let you order directly to restaurants?

    • Agent that can order for you
    • Just say "order chicken biryani from restaurant"
    • No UI, no coupons, everything automated

  • Build something great that other companies can buy instead of building from scratch

    • Your core is software
    • Many companies' core is NOT software development
    • They can acquire your company instead of building their own

  • Quick commerce stack opportunities

  • Software SaaS for global companies

    • Companies paying in USD but could pay in INR
    • Deep tech companies

  • Most efficient tech SaaS company to build products/projects

    • Collaborate with other companies

  • Service-based company can be done, but it never gets easier

    • Can you take down giants like TCS, Wipro?

  • Build for ultra-rich - For whom time is far more important than money or discounts
