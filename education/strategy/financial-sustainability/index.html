Non-Profit Financial Sustainability Model

Last Updated: 2026-06-04

Revenue Model​

1. Cost-Recovery Credits (60-70% of Operating Costs)​

Pricing Transparency:

ActionCreditsYou PayOur CostBuffer
Basic assessment (10q)200₹20₹3 AI + ₹1 infra+₹16 ops
Practice question50₹5₹1.50 AI + ₹0.50 infra+₹3 ops
Detailed feedback100₹10₹4 AI + ₹1 infra+₹5 ops
AI tutor (1 hour)2,000₹200₹40 AI + ₹10 infra+₹150 ops
Mock interview1,000₹100₹20 AI + ₹5 infra+₹75 ops
Resume review500₹50₹20 AI + ₹5 infra+₹25 ops

Why charge more than direct costs:

  • Direct costs: 20-30%
  • Platform operations: 40-50%
  • Sustainability buffer: 10-15%
  • Expansion fund: 10-15%

ALL SURPLUS REINVESTED: Lower AI costs, expand free tier, scholarships

2. Donations (30-40% of Funding)​

Sources:

  • Individual donors (HNIs, successful alumni)
  • Corporate CSR (2% mandate in India)
  • Foundations (Gates, Omidyar, Skoll, ACT, Azim Premji)
  • Government grants (Skill India, Digital India)
  • International aid (World Bank, USAID)

Tax Benefits:

  • India: 80G (50% deduction)
  • US: 501(c)(3) (100% deduction)
  • Corporate CSR compliance

Financial Projections (24 Months)​

Month 6: Break-Even​

Operations:

  • 10,000 recruiters
  • 100,000 candidates tested
  • 2,000 paying users

Revenue:

  • Credits: ₹2L/month
  • Donations: ₹50L (one-time seed)

Costs:

  • AI/Infrastructure: ₹8L
  • Team (2 people): ₹12L
  • Operations: ₹4L
  • Total: ₹24L

Status: Break-even ✅

Month 12: Self-Sustaining​

Operations:

  • 500,000 registered
  • 100,000 free tier users
  • 10,000 paying users
  • 500 scholarship users

Revenue:

  • Credits: ₹68L/month (₹8.16 crore ARR)
  • Donations: ₹2 crore

Costs:

  • AI/Infrastructure: ₹2.4 crore
  • Team (5 people): ₹1.8 crore
  • Operations: ₹1.2 crore
  • Total: ₹5.4 crore

Surplus: ₹2.76 crore → Reinvested in AI optimization

Month 18: Scholarship Expansion​

Operations:

  • 2M registered
  • 500,000 free tier
  • 50,000 paying
  • 5,000 scholarships

Revenue:

  • Credits: ₹7.5 crore/month (₹90 crore ARR)
  • Donations: ₹10 crore

Costs:

  • AI/Infrastructure: ₹18 crore (20%, down from 30%)
  • Team (15 people): ₹5.4 crore
  • Operations: ₹2.4 crore
  • Scholarships: ₹3 crore
  • Total: ₹28.8 crore

Surplus: ₹61.2 crore → Scholarships, expansion, open-source

Month 24: Scale​

Operations:

  • 5M registered
  • 2M free tier
  • 100,000 paying
  • 50,000 scholarships

Revenue:

  • Credits: ₹20 crore/month (₹240 crore ARR)
  • Enterprise: ₹14.4 crore ARR
  • Donations: ₹30 crore

Costs:

  • AI/Infrastructure: ₹38.2 crore (15%, optimized)
  • Team (40 people): ₹12 crore
  • Operations: ₹5 crore
  • Scholarships: ₹25 crore
  • International: ₹15 crore
  • Total: ₹95.2 crore

Surplus: ₹159.2 crore → 100% reinvested

Reinvestment:

  • ₹50 crore: Expand free tier (5,000 credits/month)
  • ₹50 crore: Geographic expansion (10 countries)
  • ₹30 crore: New verticals (marketing, design, product)
  • ₹29.2 crore: 12-month operational reserve

Cost Optimization Strategy​

AI Costs (Largest Variable Cost)​

PhaseModelCost/QuestionAnnual at 100K Users
Month 1-6Claude 3.5 Sonnet API₹1.50₹24 crore (18%)
Month 6-12Fine-tuned Llama 3₹0.60₹12 crore (10%)
Month 12+Custom small models₹0.30₹6 crore (5%)

Goal: Reduce AI costs from 18% → 5% of revenue through optimization

Sustainability Timeline​

  • Month 6: Break-even (credits cover costs)
  • Month 12: Self-sustaining (surplus for reinvestment)
  • Month 18: Scholarship-funded (massive free access)
  • Month 24: Reserve fund (12-month buffer for resilience)
  • Year 3+: 60% costs covered by credits, 40% by donations (sustainable forever)

Transparency Commitments​

Public Annual Reports:

  • Every rupee accounted for
  • Donation sources and uses
  • Operational surplus allocation
  • Impact metrics (not revenue metrics)

Real-Time Dashboard:

  • Live donation counter
  • Users served (free vs paid)
  • Scholarship recipients
  • Verified salary increases
