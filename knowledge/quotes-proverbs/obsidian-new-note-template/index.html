01 Worth​

  1. My value is my existence, not my achievements.
  2. I am enough, even today, even without achievement.
  3. I have nothing to prove to anyone.
  4. I will become my own best friend.
  5. My worth does not depend on anyone else’s opinion.
  6. My parents’ pride is not my identity.
  7. I am on my own journey, not in a race.
  8. My “mistakes” are my learning.
  9. My future is different from my parents’ past.
  10. My happiness is not a selfish luxury, it is a right.

02 Boundaries​

  1. My “no” is a complete sentence.
  2. I do not have to justify every decision.
  3. My privacy is my right.
  4. I will not carry anyone else’s emotional responsibility.
  5. I can keep my distance and still love.
  6. My time and energy are limited, I choose where they go.
  7. Guilt is a feeling, not a command.
  8. My parents’ moods are not my responsibility.
  9. “What will people say” is not my compass.
  10. My boundaries are my self-care.

03 The Past​

  1. My childhood was not perfect, and that is okay.
  2. My parents are flawed human beings, not saints, not monsters.
  3. I can understand my history without getting trapped in it.
  4. What happened cannot be changed, but I will decide what it means.
  5. I am under no obligation to forgive.
  6. I am under no obligation to stay bitter either.
  7. My parents’ story is only one chapter of my story.
  8. I can learn from my past, I do not have to live in it.
  9. The people who hurt me have a story of their own.
  10. My healing is for me alone.

04 The Future​

  1. My future is still being written.
  2. My choices are mine.
  3. I will give my children the freedom I never got.
  4. My home will be a safe space.
  5. I will not raise a child like me, I will raise a new person.
  6. My legacy will not be a “successful child” but a “healthy child.”
  7. I will be honest with my partner.
  8. I will invest in myself: financially, emotionally, mentally.
  9. My future is better than my apologies.
  10. I am on the road, in no hurry to arrive.

05 This Moment​

  1. Today is a new day.
  2. I am okay, right here, right now.
  3. My breath is my anchor.
  4. My feelings are valid.
  5. I do not have to “fix” anything right now.
  6. My body is my home.
  7. My awareness is my greatest strength.
  8. I can trust myself.
  9. I am a generation’s rupture.
  10. I am the cycle breaker.
