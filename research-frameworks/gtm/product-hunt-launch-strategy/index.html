Product Hunt Launch Strategy

Product Hunt Launch Timeline​

4 Weeks Before​

  • Create Product Hunt account
  • Get "Maker" badge (ship on PH Ship first)
  • Join Product Hunt community (upvote, comment)
  • Build email list of supporters (100-500 people)

2 Weeks Before​

  • Prepare gallery (5-7 images/GIFs)
  • Write tagline (60 chars max, clear value prop)
  • Draft first comment (300-500 words: problem, solution, story)
  • Create demo video (30-60 seconds)
  • Schedule launch date (Tuesday/Wednesday optimal)

1 Week Before​

  • Recruit supporters (10-20 people for first hour)
  • Prepare Twitter thread
  • Write email to list
  • Set up Google Analytics tracking
  • Test product (ensure no bugs on launch day)

Launch Day (Hour-by-Hour)​

12:01 AM PST - Post​

  • Submit product exactly at midnight PST (get full 24h)
  • Tagline, gallery, links all ready
  • Post first comment immediately (detailed story)

Hour 1 (12-1am PST)​

Critical: First hour determines ranking

  • Message 10-20 supporters (upvote + comment)
  • Post on Twitter with link
  • Post in Slack communities
  • Goal: 20-30 upvotes in first hour

Hour 2-6 (1-7am PST)​

  • Reply to EVERY comment (within 30 min)
  • Send email to waiting list
  • Post in niche communities (Reddit, Indie Hackers)
  • Goal: 50-100 upvotes by 7am

Hour 7-12 (7am-1pm PST)​

Peak traffic: Most upvotes happen during US daytime

  • Continue replying to comments
  • Share on LinkedIn
  • Post in Facebook groups
  • Goal: Top 5 by noon

Hour 13-24 (1pm-midnight PST)​

  • Maintain engagement (reply to comments)
  • Share user feedback on Twitter
  • Thank supporters publicly
  • Goal: Top 5 product of the day

Launch Day Tactics​

Gallery Optimization​

5-7 images/GIFs:

  1. Hero image (logo + tagline)
  2. Problem statement screenshot
  3. Solution screenshot (core feature)
  4. Key benefit 1
  5. Key benefit 2
  6. Social proof (if available)
  7. Call to action

Format: 16:9 aspect ratio, 1600x900px minimum

Tagline Formula​

Format: [What it does] for [who] to [outcome]

Examples:

  • "API testing tool for developers to ship faster"
  • "AI chatbot for websites to automate support"
  • "WhatsApp reminder assistant to never forget follow-ups"

Length: 60 characters max

First Comment Template​

Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I'm [Name], maker of [Product].

**The Problem:**
[1-2 sentences on pain point you observed]

**The Solution:**
[2-3 sentences on what you built and how it works]

**Why I Built This:**
[Personal story, 2-3 sentences]

**What's Next:**
[Roadmap, 1-2 sentences]

I'd love your feedback! What features would you find most useful?

Special launch offer: [Promo code or discount]

Comment Engagement​

Reply to EVERY comment within 30 min

Good responses:

  • Thank them
  • Answer their question specifically
  • Ask follow-up question
  • Show you're listening

Bad responses:

  • Generic "Thanks!"
  • Defensive
  • Copy-paste
  • Ignoring criticism

Post-Launch (Day 2-7)​

Day 2​

  • Thank everyone (Twitter thread, PH comment)
  • Aggregate feedback
  • Triage bugs/feature requests
  • Post metrics ("We got X users, learned Y")

Day 3-7​

  • Ship quick wins from feedback
  • Write launch postmortem
  • Update Product Hunt page with new features
  • Re-engage commenters ("We shipped X that you requested!")

Metrics & Goals​

Good launch:

  • Top 10 product of the day
  • 200+ upvotes
  • 50+ comments
  • 500-1,000 signups

Great launch:

  • Top 5 product of the day
  • 500+ upvotes
  • 100+ comments
  • 2,000-5,000 signups

Exceptional launch:

  • #1 product of the day
  • 1,000+ upvotes
  • 200+ comments
  • 10,000+ signups

Common Mistakes​

  1. Launching on Friday/weekend - Low traffic
  2. No first comment - Looks unprepared
  3. Ignoring comments - Kills engagement ranking
  4. Asking for upvotes - Against PH rules, can get banned
  5. Not replying fast - Slow replies hurt ranking
  6. Bad timing (posting at 3pm) - Lose half the day
  7. No email list - Need initial momentum
