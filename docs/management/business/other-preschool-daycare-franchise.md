## DPS Franchise

Securing a Delhi Public School (DPS) franchise **requires direct collaboration with the [DPS Society](https://dpsfamily.org/) headquarters in New Delhi**. Obtaining this prestigious brand affiliation involves a rigorous approval process and significant capital investment. [[1](https://erocon.in/delhi-public-school-franchise-process/), [2](https://www.naturenurture.org/dps-franchise)]

Key Requirements & Financials

- **Land Requirement:** Minimum of 5 to 8 acres (depending on the location and specific Society guidelines).
- **Built-Up Area:** At least 60,000 sq. ft. for Phase-1 construction, scaling up as student capacity grows.
- **Franchise Fees:** A one-time brand license fee is generally around ₹50 Lakhs.
- **Royalty/Annual Fees:** Unlike many other educational brands, the DPS Society usually does not take a percentage of your revenue. Instead, they charge an annual lump-sum training and guidance fee based on student volume (e.g., ₹25–30 Lakhs per year). [[1](https://erocon.in/delhi-public-school-franchise-process/)]

Step-by-Step Application Process

1. **Business Entity Setup:** You must form a registered Society, Trust, or Section-8 company. (Proprietorships are not accepted).
2. **Submit Proposal:** Send a detailed Project Report to the DPS Society HQ. This must include promoter profiles, land ownership deeds, funding proof, and a location feasibility report.
3. **Application Fee:** Pay a non-refundable processing fee (typically ranging between ₹1 Lakh to ₹5 Lakhs).
4. **Site Inspection:** A committee from the DPS Society will conduct a physical feasibility and site inspection.
5. **MOU & Construction:** Once shortlisted, you will sign a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) and begin infrastructure development strictly aligned with DPS architectural and educational standards.
6. **Final Agreement:** After construction and finalizing facilities, the final franchise agreement is signed to launch the school. [[1](https://scale100x.ai/blog/dps-franchise-cost-india-2026), [2](https://erocon.in/delhi-public-school-franchise-process/)]

_Note: The DPS Society is highly selective and typically grants affiliations to experienced educational entrepreneurs, established trusts, or corporate groups with a strong track record_
