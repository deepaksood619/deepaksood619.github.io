## B2C AI Product GTM Overview

**Target:** Consumers, solopreneurs, micro-businesses
**Model:** Freemium (generous free tier → viral growth → paid conversion)
**Timeline:** Month 1-12

## Pre-Launch

- [ ] Landing page with waitlist
- [ ] Generous free tier (50-100 uses/month)
- [ ] No credit card for free tier
- [ ] 2-minute signup (email only, no verification)

## Launch Strategy

**Week 1: Product Hunt**

- Target: #1 Product of the Day
- Timing: Tuesday/Wednesday
- Ask: "Simple, useful, free to try"

**Week 2-4: Social + Communities**

- Twitter: Demo videos, use cases
- Reddit: r/InternetIsBeautiful, r/SideProject, niche subreddits
- Facebook groups: Target audience communities

## Viral Growth Loops

1. **Free tier → Share to unlock**
   - "Tweet to get 50 extra credits"
   - "Invite 3 friends for Pro feature"

2. **Output branding**
   - "Generated with [YourTool]" watermark
   - Easy sharing (one-click to Twitter/LinkedIn)

3. **Referral program**
   - Give $10 credit, Get $10 credit
   - Track with unique links

## Conversion Funnel

**Free users → Power users → Paid**

- Month 1: 2-3% convert
- Month 3: 5-7% convert
- Month 6: 10-15% convert

**Upgrade triggers:**

- Hit free tier limit
- Unlock premium features
- Remove watermark/branding
- Priority processing (faster AI)

## Pricing

**Free:** 50-100 uses/month, all features, watermarked
**Pro:** $10-20/month, unlimited, no watermark, priority
**Business:** $50+/month, white-label, API access

## Distribution Channels

1. Product Hunt (launch)
2. Twitter/X (demos, viral content)
3. TikTok/Instagram (short demos)
4. Reddit (niche communities)
5. Facebook groups (target audiences)
6. YouTube (tutorial videos)

## Month 1-3 Goals

- 5,000+ free users
- 100-300 paid users
- 5-10% conversion rate
- $1-3K MRR
