### Import Duty

The import duty on cars in India depends on the car's value and whether it's new or used:

- **New cars:** Cars with a Cost, Insurance, and Freight (CIF) value of more than $40,000 are subject to a 100% customs tax, while those valued at less than $40,000 are subject to a 60% import duty
- **Used cars:** The import tax on used cars in India is 125%

## Car Maintenance

### 10 GOOD AC USAGE TIPS

1. USE RE-CIRCULATION MODE - For best AC effectiveness, always use Re-circulation Mode
2. SWITCH ON AC IN LOW MODE - Always switch ON the AC in LOW mode and gradually increase the speed
3. PARK IN SHADED REGION - Prefer to park your car in a shaded region
4. USE FRONT SHADES - Try to use front shades for the windshield to keep your car cabin cooler when parked in the hot sun
5. MAINTAIN PLEASANT SMELL - At the end of the trip, keep the blower ON & the fan speed at level 3 or more for a few seconds & then switch OFF the engine. This will help maintain a pleasant smell in the car
6. USE HONDA GENUINE REGRIGERANT - Always use Honda Genuine refrigerant
7. SWITCH OFF AC BEFORE ENGINE - Always switch OFF the Ac before switching OFF The engine
8. PERIODIC MAINTENANCE - Follow the Periodic Maintenance schedule religiously
9. ADJUST TEMPERATURE & BLOWER SPEED - Adjust the AC temperature & the blower speed depending upon the number of passengers in the car
10. OPEN ALL WINDOWS TO REMOVE HOT AIR - If parked in the hot sun for long, open all the windows completely to vent the hot air out before switching On the AC
