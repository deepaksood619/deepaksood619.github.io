## Business Overview

**Company Profile:**

Bosch Limited is India's largest automotive component supplier and a subsidiary of Robert Bosch GmbH, Germany. Established in 1951, the company has operations across automotive aftermarket, mobility solutions, consumer goods, and energy & building technology. Headquartered in Bangalore, Karnataka.

**Business Model:**

- **Revenue Streams:**
  - Automotive aftermarket (service parts, workshops): ~40%
  - Mobility solutions (automotive components for OEMs): ~35%
  - Consumer goods (power tools): ~15%
  - Energy & Building Technology: ~10%

- **Key Products/Services:**
  - Fuel injection systems (diesel & gasoline)
  - Automotive electronics (ABS, airbags, sensors)
  - Starter motors and alternators
  - Power tools (drills, grinders)
  - Security systems and thermotechnology

- **Distribution:**
  - Direct supply to OEMs (Maruti, Hyundai, Tata Motors, M&M)
  - Network of 350+ authorized service centers
  - Retail presence for power tools and consumer goods

**Market Position:**

- **Market Rank:** #1 in India by revenue among auto component suppliers
- **Key Competitors:** Motherson Sumi, Endurance Technologies, ZF Wabco, Valeo India

**Competitive Moat:**

- **Strong Technology Moat:** Access to Robert Bosch's global R&D and cutting-edge automotive technology gives significant advantage in complex electronics and fuel systems
- **Brand Reputation:** 75+ years in India, trusted brand in aftermarket with strong dealer loyalty
- **Switching Costs:** High integration with OEM production lines makes it difficult for automakers to switch suppliers for critical components like fuel injection and braking systems

**Management Quality:**

- **Leadership:** Soumitra Bhattacharya (Managing Director & CEO) - 35+ years in automotive industry
- **Track Record:** Consistent profitability maintained through multiple auto cycles, successfully navigated BS-VI transition, expanding into EV components
- **Concerns:** Limited disclosure on EV strategy specifics, relatively slow revenue growth compared to peers

**Corporate Governance:**

- **Promoter Holding:** 70.54% (Robert Bosch GmbH, Germany) - Strong and stable
- **Promoter Pledge:** 0.00% - Excellent
- **Institutional Support:** FII 7.22%, DII 15.05% - Adequate institutional confidence
