## Elementary Functions

1. **Polynomials**

    y = 3 x^2^ + 5x - 1

2. **Trigonometric functions**

    y = sin x; y = cos x; y = tan x

3. **Exponential functions**

    y = e^x^ , y = 10^x^

4. **Logarithmic functions**

    y = log x; y = log~2~ x
