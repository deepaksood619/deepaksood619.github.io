## Business Model

### Core Model: Cost-Per-Click Job Aggregator

Indeed operates as a **job search aggregator**, crawling company career pages, partner job boards, and direct employer postings. This is fundamentally different from Naukri's direct listings model.

**Revenue Streams:**

1. **Sponsored Jobs (Primary Revenue):** Employers pay on a cost-per-click (CPC) or cost-per-application basis for higher visibility in search results. Organic (free) listings also exist.

2. **Indeed Apply:** Streamlined application system embedded on employer career pages; charges per qualified application.

3. **Resume Services:** Candidate profiles, resume storage, optional premium features for job seekers.

4. **Employer Branding:** Company pages, employer brand advertising.

**Key Differentiator vs. Naukri:** Indeed's aggregator model means it surfaces jobs from company websites that employers haven't specifically listed on Indeed - giving it broader coverage than direct-listings platforms. This makes Indeed a "meta search engine for jobs" rather than a proprietary job database.

**Evidence Quality:** Medium-High - Indeed's model is well-documented in global HR-tech literature; India-specific disclosures are limited

## India Market Position

### Scale Metrics (Limited Data Available)

Indeed does not break out India-specific metrics in public disclosures. Recruit Holdings' Japan-based reporting does not segment India separately.

**Available India Signals:**

- Indeed operates as a default starting point for job seekers who want to scan the full market before narrowing to specific platforms
- Serves as a cross-reference tool used alongside Naukri and LinkedIn in India
- Indeed India launched aggressive localization efforts starting ~2015-2016
- Growing steadily, but not a dominant single-platform player for India-specific jobs
- Career advice content: Indeed India publishes guides (ATS resumes, cover letters) indicating active content marketing for India market

**Market Position Estimate (Unverified):**

- Naukri: India market leader (~80% traffic share)
- LinkedIn Jobs: Strong for professional/senior roles
- Indeed India: Significant but secondary player; strong as an aggregator for completeness
- Foundit: Competitor in similar tier to Indeed for India

**Evidence Quality:** Low - specific India market share data not available; estimate from secondary sources

## India-Specific Strategy

### Localization

- **Language:** Indeed India operates in English; limited regional language support (less localized than Naukri)
- **Content:** India-specific career advice (resume tips, ATS guides, interview prep)
- **Entry-level coverage:** Indeed aggregates Naukri/LinkedIn listings + company career pages; therefore has broad freshers coverage even without a dedicated fresher product

### Aggregator Advantage

Indeed's aggregator model is uniquely valuable for India because:

1. Many Indian companies post jobs on their own career pages (not just Naukri/LinkedIn)
2. Government and PSU jobs not always on job boards; Indeed's crawling surfaces them
3. International companies with India offices often post first on global career sites; Indeed surfaces these

**Evidence Quality:** Medium - logical inference from aggregator model, verified against India HR-tech market knowledge

## Competitive Positioning vs. Naukri

| Dimension | Indeed India | Naukri.com |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| Business model | Aggregator (CPC) | Direct listings (subscription) |
| India-first features | Limited | Extensive (India-built) |
| Resume database | Smaller proprietary DB | 82 million resumes (India's largest) |
| Fresher features | Generic | Naukri Campus dedicated product |
| Employer relationships | Pay-per-click | Subscription-based partnerships |
| Data network | Global coverage | India-deep |
| Hiring index/analytics | Not India-specific | Naukri JobSpeak (India's leading index) |

**Assessment:** Indeed is complementary to Naukri for Indian job seekers (broader coverage) but does not threaten Naukri's dominant position. Most serious India job seekers use both.

**Evidence Quality:** Medium - comparative positioning from market research, not primary studies

## Strategic Implications for Our Startup

**Indeed is a peripheral player in India's education-to-employment strategy.**

- Indeed's aggregator model means our graduates' profiles will appear on Indeed if we list them on Naukri/company career pages - no separate strategy needed
- No specific partnership value with Indeed vs Naukri for entry-level placement strategy
- Indeed India should be included in job search guidance for our graduates but is not a primary channel
